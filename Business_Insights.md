# 📊 Business Insights & Recommendations

This document summarizes the key findings and business recommendations derived from the **Olist E-Commerce Business Intelligence Dashboard**. The analysis focuses on identifying sales trends, product performance, seller contribution, and operational opportunities based on the available data.

---

# Executive Summary

The analysis reveals a growing e-commerce business with **13.59 million** in total revenue generated from approximately **99 thousand orders** placed by **96 thousand unique customers**.

Revenue is concentrated within a limited number of product categories and sellers, while shipping costs differ considerably across categories. These findings highlight opportunities to strengthen top-performing segments while optimizing operational efficiency.

---

# Key Findings

## 1. Revenue Is Concentrated in a Small Number of Product Categories

### Observation

The **Health & Beauty** category generated the highest revenue, followed by:

- Watches & Gifts
- Bed Bath Table
- Sports & Leisure
- Computers & Accessories

Together, these categories contribute a substantial share of total sales.

### Business Impact

Revenue is primarily driven by the marketplace's leading product categories, with a noticeable performance gap between the top and lower-ranked categories.

### Recommendation

- Continue investing in these high-performing categories.
- Expand complementary products around them.
- Diversify revenue sources by improving mid-performing categories.

---

## 2. Sales Performance Is Highly Concentrated Among Top Sellers

### Observation

The top 10 sellers generated approximately **1.79 million** in revenue.

The highest-performing seller generated approximately **229.5K**, while the tenth generated approximately **135.2K**.

### Business Impact

A relatively small number of sellers contributes a significant portion of marketplace revenue.

### Recommendation

- Strengthen partnerships with high-performing sellers.
- Identify best practices from top sellers.
- Support mid-tier sellers through promotional programs.

---

## 3. Health & Beauty Leads Revenue but Not Shipping Cost

### Observation

Although **Health & Beauty** is the highest revenue category, **Bed Bath Table** records the highest freight cost.

### Business Impact

The most profitable categories are not necessarily the most expensive to deliver.

This suggests that product characteristics such as weight or package size have a greater impact on logistics costs than revenue alone.

### Recommendation

- Review shipping contracts for categories with high freight expenses.
- Optimize packaging where possible.
- Monitor logistics efficiency by category.

---

## 4. Revenue Shows Strong Growth Throughout Most of the Analysis Period

### Observation

Monthly revenue increased steadily throughout 2017 before reaching a peak of approximately **1.01 million** in **November 2017**.

The sharp decline at the end of the timeline is caused by incomplete data for the final month rather than a genuine business decline.

### Business Impact

The business experienced sustained growth during the observed period.

### Recommendation

- Investigate the factors behind the strong performance during late 2017.
- Prepare inventory and marketing campaigns ahead of historically strong months.
- Avoid interpreting incomplete reporting periods as performance declines.

---

## 5. São Paulo Dominates Revenue Generation

### Observation

The state of **São Paulo (SP)** generated by far the highest revenue, significantly outperforming every other state.

Rio de Janeiro and Minas Gerais ranked second and third, respectively.

### Business Impact

Revenue is geographically concentrated.

### Recommendation

- Continue strengthening market presence in São Paulo.
- Explore opportunities to grow sales in lower-performing states through localized marketing campaigns.

---

## 6. Customer Satisfaction Remains High

### Observation

The average review score is **4.09 / 5**, indicating generally positive customer feedback.

### Business Impact

Customers appear satisfied with their purchasing experience.

### Recommendation

- Maintain current service quality.
- Investigate lower-rated orders to identify recurring issues.
- Use positive customer feedback as a marketing advantage.

---

## 7. Product Pricing Reflects a Mid-Market Position

### Observation

The average product price is approximately **120.65**, suggesting that the marketplace primarily serves the mid-price segment.

### Business Impact

The pricing strategy targets a broad customer base rather than focusing exclusively on premium products.

### Recommendation

- Maintain competitive pricing.
- Evaluate opportunities to introduce higher-margin premium products without affecting the core customer base.

---

# Strategic Recommendations

Based on the analysis, the following priorities are recommended:

1. Continue investing in the highest-performing product categories.
2. Improve logistics efficiency for categories with high freight costs.
3. Reduce dependency on a limited number of top sellers by strengthening mid-performing sellers.
4. Expand market share outside São Paulo through regional marketing initiatives.
5. Preserve customer satisfaction by continuously monitoring review scores.
6. Monitor seasonal demand patterns to optimize inventory planning.

---

# Conclusion

This analysis demonstrates how business intelligence can transform transactional e-commerce data into actionable business insights.

Through data cleaning, relational modeling, DAX calculations, and interactive dashboards, decision-makers can better understand sales performance, product contribution, logistics costs, and seller performance, enabling more informed strategic decisions.
